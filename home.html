About Sci³

Sci³ (Sci Cube) is a space where curiosity meets creativity.
Representing Science × Innovation × Inquiry, our club encourages students to explore, experiment, and think beyond textbooks.

Through projects, experiments, discussions, and competitions, SCI³ helps students become problem solvers and innovators.
At Sci Cube, science isn’t just learned — it’s experienced.
